Local College Hosts Hackathon

Date: 07 October 2025

Author: Anubhav Goyal

        A two-day hackathon was organised at ABES Engineering College, Ghaziabad.
        Many students participated and created innovative projects like mobile apps and
        websites. The event was successful and motivated students to learn more about technology.
    
        Judges appreciated the efforts of students and announced that such events will
        be conducted every year. The winning team created an app that helps in traffic monitoring.

Top 3 Teams

Team Name Project Position
Team Alpha Traffic Monitoring App 1st
Team Beta Air Quality Sensor 2nd
Team Gamma Smart Campus App 3rd
